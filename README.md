# 📊 HR Attrition Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

![Dashboard Preview](images/hr-attrition-dashboard.png)

## 📌 Project Overview

The **HR Attrition Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze employee attrition and workforce demographics. It helps HR professionals identify the key factors influencing employee turnover and supports data-driven decision-making for improving employee retention.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze overall employee attrition.
- Monitor workforce demographics and experience.
- Identify departments and job roles with high attrition.
- Understand attrition patterns based on age, gender, salary, and experience.
- Build an interactive dashboard for HR decision-making.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Overall attrition rate is **16.3%**, with **231 employees** leaving the organization.
- Employees earning **₹6–10 LPA** experienced the highest attrition.
- The **Administration** department recorded the highest employee attrition.
- Most employees belong to the **26–35 years** age group.
- Male employees have a higher attrition count than female employees.
- Attrition is highest among employees with lower years of experience.
